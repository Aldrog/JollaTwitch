TwitchTube

Twitch.tv client for Jolla devices

View project on GitHub

TwitchTube is a full-featured twitch.tv client for Sailfish OS.

With TwitchTube you can:

  • Find interesting channels either by viewer count or by game being played
  • Follow your favourite channels and games to access them later in a special "Following" section
  • Find a specific channel by it's name
  • Chat with another viewers of the same channels and Kappa

Future plans

  • Tablet interface
  • Ability to enable sound playback in minimized app (aka "radio mode")
  • Viewing channel's biography
  • Watching past broadcasts
